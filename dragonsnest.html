A prototype for an action / tower defense game in which you play as a dragon and defend your eggs against human attacks. Once the eggs you lay hatch, you can incarnate the new dragons when you die, or choose to let go of your current life to place a powerful dragon turret.

I am the gameplay programmer in charge of the camera, controller, enemies and spawn system.. My focus in on the dragon's game feel as well as consolidating my methodology, from the prototyping phase to the concrete, organised implementation.

The game will be published on Steam at the end of February! This time the goal is to make a game with a replay value, not a demo or a vertical slice.
