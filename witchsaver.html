ludum dare page · itch.io
Made for the Ludum Dare #41 in 24h, this trio of Warioware-like minigames only launches within the Microsoft Windows screensaver! When you lose, the screen wakes up, and you have to wait until it appears again.

I made everything related to the frog mini-game, and contributed to the others’ design.

WHAT I LEARNT:



I learnt how to make games inside screensavers! It was super fun.

It was a good exercise in estimating what I was able to do on my own in a set amount of time, especially while avoiding sleep deprivation.
