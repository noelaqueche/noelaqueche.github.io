Then The Tabled Laughed is a

local multiplayer game for 2 to 12 players

about some dude trying to survive in a room full of mimics. Think “Prop Hunt meets Red Light, Green Light”.

One player controls the human with the mouse - the other players can spawn up to 11 mimics amongst the furniture with a single keyboard, at any moment in game!

The game was made for the Ludum Dare #40: we ranked 15th overall, 10th in fun and 6th in innovation !

WHAT I LEARNT:



I began to adopt Object-Oriented Programming!

I discovered keyboard ghosting as a hardware limitation

I must work on simplifying my games' rules so they're easier to grasp, without losing the concept's originality.
