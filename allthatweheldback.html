allthatweheldback is a short, personal experience I made over the course of a week for the A Game By Its Cover game jam.

You are an astronaut stranded in space, about to die from the lack of oxygen; as a haunting melody plays you are encouraged to write in your travel log everything never told to others, the good and the bad. Just for yourself.

The goal was to encourage people to share what they feel with their closed ones, before it’s actually too late to do so.

WHAT I LEARNT:



I learnt how to make a chatbox in Unity.

I made this game on my free time while at my internship. It helped me realize that I need to convey meaning through my work.
